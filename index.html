About me

Welcome. I'm here as your coach, consultant and mentor.

As a Chief People Officer, I've held integral roles building and steering global leadership teams through transformational change and growth.

From founder led start-ups to complex global organisations, I have a deep understanding of organisational dynamics and their nuances. I help navigate challenges and identify opportunities, creating impact for all stakeholders.

I coach leaders to sharpen their awareness and effectiveness. I consult and mentor to create successful cultures that people want to be a part of, and where they can thrive.

I work with boards, c-suite teams, leadership teams and HR leaders. Start-ups, scale-ups, large organisations and non-profits.

I specialise in:

  • Executive and leadership one-on-one coaching programmes
  • Culture and HR strategy development
  • Mentoring HR leaders

Culture Consultancy and HR Mentoring

Growth, action and impact.

Culture is either an externally facing asset or a liability. More than small gestures, or simply a measure of how your people feel about working with you, when cultivated with care, culture is an extremely potent competitive advantage.

I help CEOs and HR Leaders develop the interplay between strategy and culture. Whether seeking specific interventions, ongoing mentoring or sweeping cultural change, I immerse myself to offer a fresh perspective for meaningful progress and sustainable impact.

I work with:

  • SMEs – new and established
  • Start-ups and scale-ups
  • Newly acquired businesses in large organisations
  • Regions and markets in large organisations
  • Non-profits and charities
