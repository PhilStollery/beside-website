Culture Consultancy and Mentoring

With a deep understanding of the intricate relationship between strategy and culture, I offer CEOs, and/or HR Leaders, invaluable insights into bridging culture gaps within their organisations.

It's more than how employees perceive their workplace or, how employers attract and retain talent. Culture is evolving into an externally facing asset or potential liability.

Culture can either bolster, or impede, the delivery of organisational strategy. As leaders create, cultivate and nurture it, culture can become a potent competitive advantage.

My approach involves a culture assessment with tailored strategies, and pragmatic interventions, to evolve and close the gaps.

Whether you’re seeking specific interventions, ongoing mentoring, or sweeping cultural transformations, I fully immerse myself beside you offering a fresh perspective facilitating meaningful change and sustainable impact.

Different organisations have different cultural challenges. Together we work out the right consultancy approach for you. I work with:

  • SMEs – new and established
  • Start-ups and scale-ups
  • Newly acquired businesses in large organisations
  • Regions and markets in large organisations
  • Non-profit and charities

A fresh look. From the outside in and the inside out.

How Beside works

I relate by placing clients at the centre and creating trust built upon compassion, clarity, and mutual candour. I bring experience, insight, and pragmatic support through a relational and person-centred coaching and consulting approach.

Compassionate communication enables clarity

Compassion is conducive for open dialogue, enhancing communication and creating clarity.

Clarity enhances candour

When the process is transparent and goals are clear, clients feel comfortable and open, meaning honest and candid conversations.

Candour deepens compassion

Authenticity and honesty. Constructive feedback and observations shared openly. I enable you to identify blind spots and make the necessary changes.

Trust

As these three elements interplay, trust grows. Clients know I maintain confidentiality and act in their best interests – always.
