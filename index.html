About me

I’m a leadership coach. I consult and mentor too.

Experience

I’ve built – and been a part of – global leadership teams through times of change and growth.

True leaders never stop learning. I’m at my best when I’m coaching growing leaders to be more aware and effective, creating cultures that people want to be a part of. And where they can thrive.

Today I work with boards and leadership teams, start-ups, scale-ups, large organisations and non- profits. I help teams – and individuals – make better leadership decisions to advance personal and shared missions.

Coach.

Or Mentor.

When I coach, I focus on personal needs and outcomes, using proven coaching methodologies and tools.

When I mentor, I share wisdom based on my own expertise and experiences.

Coaching has structure. Mentoring is more informal.

I do both.

And both have a positive impact.

How Beside works

Compassion is conducive for open dialogue, enhancing communication and creating clarity.

When the coaching process is transparent and goals are clear, clients feel comfortable and open, meaning honest and candid conversations.

Authenticity and honesty strengthen support and understanding.

As these three elements interplay, trust grows.

COMPASSION

Compassionate communication enables clarity

I take time to understand and relate to thoughts, emotions and experiences.

I spot the opportunities and respond with insight, honesty and compassion.

I uplift and motivate.

CLARITY

Clarity enhances candour

We go at the right pace – shoulder to shoulder.

We focus on the things that make a meaningful impact – to you as a leader, to your team, and to your organisation.

Through awareness and insight, new perspectives emerge.

CANDOUR

Candour deepens compassion

Candour isn’t about being blunt. It requires tact, clarity, and diplomacy.

Constructive feedback and observations shared openly. I enable you to identify blind spots and to make the changes that’ll help you grow as a leader.

It works both ways. I listen and adapt too, fostering open communication and effective partnerships.

TRUST

Trust and integrity

Clients rely on me to maintain confidentiality and act in their best interests – always.
